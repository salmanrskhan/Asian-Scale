FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

When selecting a weighing machine keep range, readability, and your requirements in mind. Figure out the minimum and maximum required capacity for your applications. Remember what range of weights you need to weigh. Find how many decimal places you need to measure. Check if you have any special requirements in terms of applications or methods.

Calibration frequency depends on the type and on the applications, but it is preferred to calibrate the weighing scale every month. You can even extend the calibration period by every quarter. Some applications need calibration after every 15 days. To get a precise idea, read the instruction manual that comes with the weighing machine.
