Who We Are

Asian Scales, established under the name of Asian Electro Weighing company, is a specialist consultants for weighing instruments of every make and model based in Haridwar, Uttarakhand, India, has been dedicated to providing ideal solutions in the industry for over 25 years. Our success can be attributed to our focus on establishing and enforcing government approved standards of weights and measures in the industry by providing advanced weighing solutions that cater to our customers' evolving needs. With a strong emphasis on quality control. Asian Scales ensures that our services meet the highest standards of accuracy and durability. This commitment to excellence has garnered a loyal customer base. Including esteemed clients such as Lotus, Surya, Hindustan Unilever, and many others Operating on a direct-to- customer (D2C) model. Asian Scales is able to offer competitive prices without compromising on the quality and level of service provided. We prioritize customer satisfaction and strive to exceed expectations at every step. Partner with Asian Scales to experience our innovative approach, expert knowledge of cutting-edge technology, and unwavering commitment to delivering exceptional weighing solutions.
